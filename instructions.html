SIMON SAYS

How to Play

Objective: Watch the sequence of colors and repeat it correctly. The sequence grows each round!


How to Play:

1. Watch the sequence of flashing colors.

2. Click the colors in the same order.

3. Each correct round adds a new color to the sequence.

4. One wrong click ends the game.


Difficulty Levels:

Easy: Slow speed, generous timer

Normal: Moderate speed

Hard: Fast speed, short timer

Special: 6 colors, random flash speed, Simon wheel rotates every 5 rounds!


Controls:

Click the colored segments, or use keyboard keys 1-4 (1-6 in Special mode).


Timer Mode: Enable in launcher settings. You have limited time per round!
